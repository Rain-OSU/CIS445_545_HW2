A Short Biography

     As previously stated, I am Rain O'Sullivan. Currently, I am a 21-year-old junior CIS Major/Creative Writing Minor, attending Bradley University. I have lived many places throughout my life, but I spent the most time in Morris, IL, where I went to Middle and High School. My parents live in a church that was built during the Civil War, and then modified into a home much later. I have one sibling, and she is 6 years older than me. She has 2 sons and 2 daughters, so I am an uncle. I have been in a committed relationship, to a woman named Lynn, for nearly 8 years, and got engaged 2 years ago. We're getting married after I graduate. I go home often to play D&D with my friends and spend time with Lynn. I use Amazon Prime far too much to buy movies and books. And that is my life story.
